Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Athlete Engine app operated by Jackson Thielen ("Service Provider"). This policy is effective as of 2026-06-01.

Information We Collect

The Application collects the following information when you register and use it:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Health and wellness data you voluntarily submit (sleep, energy, soreness, stress levels)
  • Athletic performance data (throwing logs, lifting sessions)
  • Program and team affiliation

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to provide the Application's features, including displaying your performance data to you and, where applicable, to your coaching staff within the same program. We do not sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

Third Party Access

The Application uses Firebase (Google) for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed by Firebase. No other third parties have access to your personal data.

Coaching Staff Access

If you join a program using a program code, your wellness and performance data will be visible to the coaches associated with that program. This is a core feature of the Application.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting Jacksonthielen@gmail.com or using the Delete Account option within the app.

Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Security

We use Firebase's industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the effective date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at Jacksonthielen@gmail.com.
